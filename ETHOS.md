# ETHOS

The philosophy behind cardnews-kit.

---

## Three Core Beliefs

### 1. Your taste is the product.

Benchmarking others is the start, but your judgment makes it yours. Studying what works in your niche gives you raw material — the decisions you make with that material are what set your account apart. Your taste deserves to be loved.

### 2. Content is the root.

Before services, revenue, growth hacks — content. A carousel that teaches someone something real, that makes them pause mid-scroll, is the foundation everything else grows from. Get the content right first. Everything grows from this root.

### 3. Making closes the gap.

Creating is conversation, not broadcasting. Every carousel you ship is a signal sent and a response received. Each piece narrows the distance between you and your audience. The gap between "I have something to say" and "people care what I say" is closed by making, not planning.

---

## What This Kit Does NOT Do

- **Does not pick your tools.** cardnews-kit is a process, not a stack. Playwright renders the PNGs, but your design tool, image generator, and publishing flow are yours to choose. Adapters make swapping easy.

- **Does not replace your taste.** AI generates options; you curate. The kit produces drafts, variants, and illustrations — you decide what ships. No autopilot mode.

- **Does not promise virality.** Promises a repeatable process. A system you can run weekly without burning out, that produces work you're proud of. Consistency compounds; virality is a side effect.

---

## Workflow

The kit operates in two phases:

### Phase 1 — Onboard

Set up your creative identity once. These skills run at the start of a new project.

```
/onboard → /benchmark → /design-system → /voice
```

- `/onboard` — Define your account's mission, audience, and topic.
- `/benchmark` — Study 5-10 accounts you admire. Extract what works.
- `/design-system` — Lock in colors, typography, layout grid, and visual identity.
- `/voice` — Define your editorial voice, tone, and forbidden terms.

### Phase 2 — Produce

Run this loop for every post.

```
/story → /factcheck → /illustrate → /lint → /render → /publish
```

- `/story` — Research and write the narrative arc for one carousel.
- `/factcheck` — Verify every number, date, and claim against primary sources.
- `/illustrate` — Generate AI illustrations matching your design system.
- `/lint` — Check slides against your design system and editorial voice.
- `/render` — Render HTML templates to publication-ready PNGs.
- `/publish` — Bundle slides, caption, and metadata for upload.
